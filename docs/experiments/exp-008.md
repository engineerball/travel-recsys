# Exp-008 вҖ” Fix warmup: step-based warmup_steps (2026-05-19)

## Changes from [Exp-007](exp-007.md)

| Change | Where | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| `warmup_epochs: 3` вҶ’ `warmup_steps: 450` | `configs/config.yaml` | Absolute step count; decoupled from `steps_per_epoch` |
| `warmup_steps` priority in `_build_optimizer` | `src/training/trainer.py` | Uses `cfg["warmup_steps"]` directly if present; falls back to `warmup_epochs Г— steps_per_epoch` |

**No model or data changes.**

**Motivation:** Exp-007 used `warmup_epochs=3` which scaled linearly with `steps_per_epoch`: 3 Г— 500 = 1500 warmup steps (vs 3 Г— 150 = 450 in Exp-006). The extended warmup shifted the LR ramp, hurting Hit@10 for article (вҲ’29%) and event (вҲ’16%). Setting `warmup_steps: 450` restores the original warmup length regardless of epoch size.

## Notes

- **Hit@10 recovered** вҖ” event +32% and article +30% vs Exp-007, confirming warmup length was the cause of the Exp-007 @10 regression.
- **Warmup controls a precision vs recall trade-off:**
  - Short warmup (450 steps, ~0.9 epochs) вҶ’ model reaches full LR quickly, converges to tighter local clusters вҶ’ better Hit@10.
  - Long warmup (1500 steps, 3 epochs) вҶ’ slower LR ramp, broader exploration вҶ’ better Hit@50/@100.
- **Best epoch arrived early (epoch 8)** вҖ” 450 warmup steps over 500-step epochs means full LR is reached in epoch 1. The model converges fast; patience=15 stopped at epoch 23.
- vs Exp-006: Hit@10 overall +3%, accommodation +3%, event +10%. Event @50/@100 regressed вҖ” larger FAISS catalog (1,639 vs 492 events) is the primary driver (more distractors at higher K).
- **Exp-008 is the new best Hit@10 baseline.** @50/@100 is weaker than Exp-007; if downstream use requires recall at higher K, consider a warmup between 450вҖ“1500 steps.

## Open issues

- Warmup sweet spot sits somewhere between 450 and 1500 steps. A sweep (600, 900, 1200) could find the Pareto-optimal point between @10 and @50.
- Event @50 (0.258) regressed significantly vs Exp-006 (0.315) вҖ” growing FAISS catalog is the likely cause, not model quality.
- Early stopping triggered at epoch 8 best; patience 15 means 23 total epochs. Consider reducing patience to 10 now that steps/epoch is larger.
